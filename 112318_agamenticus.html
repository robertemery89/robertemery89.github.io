Friday November 23, 2018 - Wild Turkey Day

Wild Turkey Day was created to be the antithesis of Black Friday. After careful deliberation, the creators decided the opposite of going to crowded stores to buy frivolous junk was bringing a bottle of Wild Turkey Bourbon into the woods and walking around. On this day we honored the tradition by visiting Mount Agamenticus with a bottle of bourbon to walk through the snowy woods of southern coastal Maine. There was great joy for all.
