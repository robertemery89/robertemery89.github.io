Friday August 3, 2018

Champney Falls is an awesome sight on the ascent to Chocorua from the Kancamagus Highway. Square ledges, cascades and small free falls make for a lovely stop in the woods. There's even a small lookout at the top of the falls. Once my brother and I make it to the summit we were greeted with one of the best views in the Whites with a true 360 degree perspective. See my ascent via Carter Ledge from Route 16 if you are looking for more wide open views on the way up along a ridge.
