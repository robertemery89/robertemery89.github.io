Friday November 24, 2017

Wild Turkey Day was created to be the antithesis of Black Friday. After careful deliberation, the creators decided the opposite of going to crowded stores to buy frivolous junk was bringing a bottle of Wild Turkey Bourbon into the woods and walking around. On this day we had the great honor of visiting the very woods in which the holiday was born accompanied by the founders themselves.
