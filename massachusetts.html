Massachusetts

Rob's original woods to be in. My backyard and the parks in Paxton, MA were the first woods I loved. Since them I have been lucky to explore so much more of the state. From the lush Berkshire Mountains of the west to the coastal hikes on the Cape, the state has something to offer everyone. I've discovered a wealth of waterfalls in the middle of the state and accessible hikes within a half our of Boston. There is still so much more to explore in my back yard.
