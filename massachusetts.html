Massachusetts

Rob's original woods. My backyard and the parks in Paxton, MA were the first woods I loved. Since then I have been lucky to explore so much more. From the lush Berkshire Mountains of the west to the coastal hikes on the Cape, the state has something to offer everyone. I've discovered a wealth of waterfalls in the middle of the state and accessible hikes within a half our of Boston. There is still so much more to explore in my back yard.
