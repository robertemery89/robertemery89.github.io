Saturday October 7, 2017

My brother-in-law enjoyed this easy to moderate hike through the pretty woods of Pawtuckaway State Forest. A fairly small climb brought us to some nice lookouts in addition to some interesting rocks. The descent was leisurely and brought us to at least one pond and another bog. Overall, this is a solid spot to get in some woods.
