Saturday March 21, 2020

I love this hike! After a flat half mile I was at the lower falls, the highlight of the hike. From there I walked upstream along the river at a gentle slope. There are so many places to stop, have a snack and enjoy the scenery. On this hike, I forgot my hiking boots and learned that Crocs with a good pair of socks can be a very servicable hiking shoe.
