Sunday June 9, 2019

This was a nice hike through the Blue Hills Reservation south of Boston. My nephew, brother, ans sister-in-law enjoyed a walk up the Skyline Trail to eventually enjoy some views of the city (I'm sorry I don't have pictures of it). The Blue Hills offer a wealth of trails ranging from short walks around a pond to moderate hikes where you can get some elevation gain by goin up and down the hills.
