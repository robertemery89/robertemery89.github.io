Mount Sunapee and Lake Solitude

Highlights

Incredible alpine lake
Lake Solitude lives up to its name
Listening to the snow falling off the trees surrounding the lake
Amazing view of the lake and beyond from a ledge
One of the best pound for pound views in New England (two miles to an alpine lake and another ¼ mile to extended unobstructed views)

Challenges

The final ascent to Sunapee is not needed and requires hiking along a ski trail
Bring yak tracks if you plan to hike this in the early/mid spring

6 miles

Wednesday April 1, 2020

This early spring hike was right after a couple inches of fresh powder in the mountains. Less than two hours from Boston, this hike provided amazing scenery at Lake Solitude and from the view looking down upon it. Don't bother hiking to the ski summit since the views are no better. This was a terrific way to say goodbye to winter.
