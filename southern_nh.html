Southern New Hampshire

This region of New England is often overlooked. It offers much more than Mount Mondadnock and is a great place to get a head start in the spring while it's still winter in the White Mountains. I've been surpised by the amzing scenery including waterfalls, lakes, and distant views of Boston. If you are looking for an easy way to escape Boston and explore the woods this is yours spot.
