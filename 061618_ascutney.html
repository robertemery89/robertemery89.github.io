Saturday June 16, 2018

Mount Ascutney has some very pretty views atop its peak. The hike itself is beautiful, especially near the summit. The most unique part of the hike was watching many hang gliders swirling the skies. Gerry Falls was not much of anything during my visit but is probably a pretty stop in the spring or after rain.
