Saturday February 22, 2020

This was only my second ever winter hike and the first for my partner. We used yaktracks over waterproof boots and were constantly relayering, but had a fun time. The snow covered terrain was gorgeous. The views from the two peaks to Squam Lake were awesome. The alpine path connecting the two might have been even more beautiful. For our next winter hike we definitely need to get some sleds for the hike down, we tried just using our butts with not much luck.
