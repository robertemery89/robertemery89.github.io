My partner and I stayed at a Tiny House, rented by Getaway. It was a cool, cozy, winter experience. We brought our cross country skis and skied right from the doorsteps of our tiny home and to the Epsom State Forest for a little adventure. It had just snowed which made the experience even more magical.
