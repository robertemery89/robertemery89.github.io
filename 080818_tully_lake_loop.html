Tulley lake is a a pretty place in north-central Mass. Doane's Falls and Spirit Falls are a short hike upstream from the shores (check them out here). I don't recomend this specific hike because it was a bit long and the scenery did not change much. Tulley Lake Campground is located in the northeast of the ake and rents canoes and kayaks. A fun weekend would be staying at the campground, hiking to Doane's Falls and Spirit Falls for one day, paddling for another, and driving to Royalston Falls or Tulley Mountain for a third.
