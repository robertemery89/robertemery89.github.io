Sunday December 23, 2018

This little park is behind where I grew up. It consists of a short loop and follows Stony Brook for much of the way. It is a great place to go on a walk in the woods, trail run, or to bring your dog swimming. My dog Emma loved this place just as I did. When I struggled with depression, I started healthy habits by running along this loop.
This hike was our last together. She loved the woods more than I did and wanted to come to this park, play fetch in the water, and bring home the biggest stick she could carry. If you live in the area, bring your dog and enjoy the quaint little park that is Stony Brook.
